This tool is for compression and easy transfer of files for the Zandpack code.
Usage:
     (compression and upload): 
            zzz Dir=$PWD folder=tmp files=* command="rsync -a tmp.tar.xz name@clusterlogin.dk:/dir/upload/" delete_all=True
     (decompression): 
            zzz Dir=$PWD folder=tmp.tar.xz mode=decompress
default options:
      {'folder': 'tmp', 'files': '{}', 'mode': 'compress', 'delete': 'False', 'command': 'None', 'delete_all': 'False'}
